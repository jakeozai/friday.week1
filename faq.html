Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Sixth Sense?

Sixth Sense is a service that generates new lotto numbers every day for your luck. You can get 6 lucky numbers with a simple button click.

How are the lotto numbers generated?

The numbers are generated by randomly selecting 6 numbers from 1 to 45. All numbers are extracted through a fair and random algorithm.

Is this service free?

Yes, the Sixth Sense lotto number generator is completely free to use.
